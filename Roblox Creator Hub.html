    Data ModelEvery place is represented by a data model, a hierarchy of objects that describe everything about the place.
    CollaborationCreating a high-quality experience demands many skills such as modeling, scripting, user interface design, and audio production.
    AssetsAlmost everything in Roblox is represented as a cloud-based asset with a unique corresponding ID.
    WorkspaceWorkspace is a container service that holds objects that you want the Roblox engine to render in the 3D world.
    ScriptingScripting lets you add custom, dynamic behavior to your experiences, providing more engaging and immersive interactions for your users.
    Lighting & EffectsThe Lighting and SoundService container services let you control and customize environmental effects.
    PlayersWhen a user joins an experience, Roblox represents them as a Player in the data model.
    InputEvery experience needs to receive user input in some way to allow users to interact and view their environment.
